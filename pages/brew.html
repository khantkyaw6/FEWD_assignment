Brewing Equipment

French Press

Perfect for a rich and bold cup. Add coarse grounds, pour hot water, steep for 4 mins, and press!

Espresso Machine

Make professional espresso at home. Tamp your grounds well and extract under pressure for crema-rich shots.

Pour Over

For clarity and flavor. Use a gooseneck kettle and bloom the grounds before continuing the pour.

AeroPress

Great for travel. Combine fine coffee and water, stir, and press in under two minutes for a clean cup.
